Our Story

Conviction

The Beginning

Design-Driven.
From Day One.

Vantello was founded on a single observation: that the Indian home deserved bathroom fittings built to its actual demands — hard water, humid climates, heavy daily use, and the aspiration for true luxury.

We spent years studying premium bathroom brands globally before designing our first product. The result is a range that performs as beautifully as it looks.

Our Promise

Imported Quality.
Indian Access.

Vantello occupies the position that ambitious buyers seek: the refinement and performance of global premium brands, accessible through an Indian company that understands Indian homes.

Dealers trust us. Architects specify us. Homeowners love us. Because we refuse to compromise.
